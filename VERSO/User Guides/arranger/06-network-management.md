# Arranger Guide: Network Management

Build and manage your network of introducers and partners.

![My Introducers](./screenshots/19-my-introducers.png)
*The My Introducers page showing your introducer network.*

---

## Overview

Your **Network** includes all introducers and partners who help distribute your deals. Managing this network is essential for:
- Tracking referral relationships
- Setting up fee plans
- Processing commissions
- Monitoring performance

---

## Accessing Your Network

The arranger sidebar has separate pages for each network type:

| Sidebar Item | Purpose |
|--------------|---------|
| **My Partners** | Strategic partnership firms |
| **My Introducers** | Individual referral partners |
| **My Commercial Partners** | Distribution platforms |
| **My Lawyers** | Legal partners |

Click on any of these in the left sidebar to manage that network segment.

---

## Network Member Types

### Introducers

Individuals or firms who:
- Refer investors to your deals
- Receive commissions on successful subscriptions
- Have signed introducer agreements

### Partners

Strategic relationships who:
- May co-invest or co-arrange
- Have broader partnership terms
- Often higher commission structures

### Commercial Partners

Distribution platforms who:
- Have investor networks
- Place your deals with their clients
- Operate under placement agreements

## Adding to Your Network

### Step 1: Initiate Addition

1. Click **"+ Add to Network"**
2. Select type: Introducer, Partner, or Commercial Partner

### Step 2: Enter Details

| Field | Description | Required |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| **Name** | Full legal name | Yes |
| **Company** | Their organization | If applicable |
| **Email** | Primary contact email | Yes |
| **Phone** | Contact number | Recommended |
| **Type** | Introducer/Partner/Commercial | Yes |

### Step 3: Agreement Type

Select the agreement type:
- Standard Introducer Agreement
- Custom Agreement (upload)
- Partner Agreement
- Placement Agreement

### Step 4: Send Invitation

1. Review details
2. Click **"Send Invitation"**
3. They receive email with agreement
4. Status shows as `pending`

---

## Agreement Signing

### The Process

1. Network member receives email
2. Reviews agreement terms
3. Signs electronically
4. Agreement becomes active
5. They're added to your network

### Agreement Status

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| `pending` | Awaiting signature |
| `active` | Signed and active |
| `expired` | Agreement term ended |
| `terminated` | Relationship ended |

## Agreement Management

### Viewing Agreements

1. Go to member profile
2. Click **"Agreements"** section
3. View or download signed agreements

### Agreement Renewal

When agreements expire:
1. System sends renewal notice
2. You can send new agreement
3. Member signs to renew
4. Relationship continues

### Agreement Termination

To end a relationship:
1. Go to member profile
2. Click **"..."** → **"Terminate Agreement"**
3. Provide reason
4. Confirm termination
5. Member cannot make new introductions

## Integration with Fee Plans

### Linking to Deals

When a network member has:
1. Active agreement
2. Accepted fee plan for a deal

They can:
- Make introductions
- Track their referrals
- Accrue commissions

### No Fee Plan Scenario

Without an accepted fee plan:
- They're in your network
- But no commission tracking for that deal
- Must set up fee plan first

---

## Common Issues

### "Member not receiving invitation"

1. Check email address is correct
2. Ask them to check spam folder
3. Resend invitation
4. Use alternative email

### "Agreement expired but still working together"

1. Send renewal agreement
2. They sign to reactivate
3. Continues seamlessly

### "Network member left their company"

1. Update their profile
2. May need new agreement
3. Discuss commission arrangements

---

## Best Practices

### Onboard Properly

When adding network members:
- Explain the platform
- Provide training on making introductions
- Clear expectations on reporting

### Regular Check-ins

Maintain relationships:
- Quarterly performance reviews
- Update on new deals
- Address any concerns

### Document Everything

Keep records of:
- All agreements (stored on platform)
- Communications
- Special arrangements
