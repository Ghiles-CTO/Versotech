# Partner Guide: Partnership Agreement

Learn about the partnership agreement that formalizes your strategic relationship.

---

## Overview

The **Partnership Agreement** establishes your strategic relationship with an arranger. It covers broader terms than a standard introducer agreement.

---

## Partnership vs Introducer Agreement

| Aspect | Partnership | Introducer |
|--------|-------------|------------|
| **Scope** | Strategic collaboration | Referral activities |
| **Terms** | Often longer term | Standard terms |
| **Exclusivity** | May include | Rarely |
| **Commission** | Negotiated structure | Standard rates |

---

## Receiving the Agreement

### How You're Notified

1. Email with agreement link
2. Platform notification
3. Dashboard shows pending

### What It Includes

- Partnership terms
- Scope of relationship
- Commission framework
- Confidentiality terms
- Duration and termination

---

## Reviewing the Agreement

### Key Sections

| Section | Focus On |
|---------|----------|
| **Scope** | What the partnership covers |
| **Exclusivity** | Any exclusive arrangements |
| **Commission** | Framework (specifics in fee plans) |
| **Term** | Duration, renewal |
| **Termination** | How either party can exit |

### Before Signing

- Read complete agreement
- Note any concerns
- Discuss with legal if needed
- Understand obligations

---

## Signing Process

### Step 1: Access Agreement

From email or platform:
1. Click signing link
2. Review full document

### Step 2: Apply Signature

1. Navigate to signature fields
2. Sign using preferred method
3. Complete any additional fields

### Step 3: Submit

1. Review all entries
2. Click "Complete Signing"
3. Receive confirmation

---

## After Signing

### Immediate Effects

- Agreement becomes active
- You're in arranger's network
- Can receive fee plans for deals
- Partnership activities can begin

### Document Storage

Your signed agreement is stored in:
- **Documents** section
- Always accessible
- Reference for terms

---

## Agreement Management

### Checking Status

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| `pending` | Awaiting signature |
| `active` | Signed and active |
| `expired` | Term ended |
| `terminated` | Relationship ended |

### Renewal

If agreement has a term:
- Notification before expiry
- Renewal agreement sent
- Sign to continue
