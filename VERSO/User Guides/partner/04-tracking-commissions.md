# Partner Guide: Tracking Commissions

Monitor your partnership commissions from accrual to payment.

---

## Overview

Your commissions are tracked automatically based on your accepted fee plans and the deals that close.

---

## Commission Lifecycle

```
Deal Closes → Commission Accrues → Invoice Requested → Submitted → Paid
```

### Status Flow

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| `accrued` | Earned, awaiting invoice |
| `invoice_requested` | Submit invoice now |
| `invoice_submitted` | Under review |
| `invoiced` | Approved |
| `paid` | Complete |
| `cancelled` | Commission was cancelled |
| `rejected` | Invoice/commission rejected |

> **Note**: Commissions may be cancelled if the underlying subscription is cancelled. Rejections require review and resubmission.

---

## Accessing Commissions

### Navigation

1. Go to **Commissions**
2. See all your commissions
3. Filter by status, deal

### Dashboard Cards

| Card | Shows |
|------|-------|
| **Total Earned** | Lifetime commissions |
| **Pending** | Awaiting payment |
| **This Month** | Recent activity |

---

## Commission Details

### What You See

For each commission:
- Deal name
- Related subscription/volume
- Commission amount
- Status
- Payment timeline

### Calculation

Based on your fee plan:
```
Volume: $2,000,000
Rate: 1.5%
Commission: $30,000
```

---

## Partner Commission Structures

### Co-Arrangement Fees

If you co-arrange:
- May receive arrangement fees
- Plus distribution commissions
- Tracked separately

### Trailing Commissions

For ongoing arrangements:
- Annual payments
- Based on AUM
- Tracked over time

---

## Tracking Multiple Deals

### Deal-by-Deal View

1. Go to **Commissions**
2. Select "By Deal"
3. See breakdown per deal

### Aggregate View

Dashboard shows:
- Total across all deals
- Status breakdown
- Pending actions
