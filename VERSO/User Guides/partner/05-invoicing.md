# Partner Guide: Invoicing

Submit invoices to receive your commission payments.

---

## Overview

When commissions reach `invoice_requested` status, submit your invoice through the platform to receive payment.

---

## Invoice Requirements

### Required Elements

| Element | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Your Details** | Company name, address, tax ID |
| **Invoice Number** | Your reference |
| **Date** | Invoice date |
| **Recipient** | Arranger details |
| **Description** | Partnership commission for [deal] |
| **Amount** | Exact commission amount |
| **Bank Details** | Payment instructions |

### Tax Considerations

Include as applicable:
- VAT/GST registration
- Tax amounts
- Withholding acknowledgment

---

## Submitting Invoices

### Step 1: Access Commission

1. Go to **Commissions**
2. Find `invoice_requested` items
3. Click to open

### Step 2: Upload Invoice

1. Click "Submit Invoice"
2. Upload PDF document
3. Verify amount matches
4. Submit

### Step 3: Track Status

After submission:
- Status changes to `invoice_submitted`
- Arranger reviews
- Approved or rejected

---

## Payment Processing

### After Approval

1. Status: `invoiced`
2. Payment queued
3. Processed per terms
4. Funds transferred

### Timeline

| Stage | Typical Timing |
|-------|----------------|
| Review | 1-7 days |
| Approval | 1-3 days |
| Payment | 30 days |

---

## Handling Issues

### Rejection

If invoice rejected:
1. Review reason
2. Correct issue
3. Resubmit

### Common Issues

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Wrong amount | Match to commission exactly |
| Missing info | Add required details |
| Wrong format | Use proper invoice format |

---

## Record Keeping

### What to Maintain

- Copies of all invoices
- Payment confirmations
- Commission statements

### For Tax Purposes

Keep records for:
- Income reporting
- Tax filing
- Audit trail
