# Partner Guide: Fee Plans

Understand and manage fee plans for your partnership deals.

---

## Overview

Even with a partnership agreement, you receive **deal-specific fee plans** that define your commission for each opportunity.

---

## Fee Plan Basics

### Deal-Specific

Each deal has its own fee plan:
- Specific rates for that deal
- May vary between deals
- Accept before earning commission

### Commission Basis

Commission is always on **funded amount**:
```
Investor funds: $1,000,000
Your rate: 2%
Commission: $20,000
```

---

## Receiving Fee Plans

### Notification

When arranger creates fee plan:
1. Email notification
2. Platform notification
3. Shows in Fee Plans section

### What You See

- Deal details
- Commission rate/structure
- Payment timing
- Accept/reject options

---

## Fee Plan Structures

### Common Structures for Partners

| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Flat Rate** | Single percentage |
| **Tiered** | Volume-based rates |
| **Carry Share** | Participation in performance |
| **Hybrid** | Combination structures |

### Example: Tiered + Trailing

```
Upfront: 1.5% at close
Trail: 0.25% annually for fund life
```

---

## Accepting Fee Plans

### Review First

Check:
- Rates align with partnership terms
- Structure is as discussed
- Deal is one you want to work on

### Acceptance Process

1. Click on fee plan
2. Review all terms
3. Click "Accept Fee Plan"
4. Confirm

### After Acceptance

- Fee plan becomes active
- Commission tracks automatically
- Deal appears in your active list

---

## Managing Fee Plans

### Viewing All

Go to **Fee Plans**:
- See all by status
- Filter by deal, status
- Track acceptance

### Status Meanings

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| `sent` | Awaiting your response |
| `accepted` | Active |
| `rejected` | You declined |

---

## Negotiating Terms

### If Terms Don't Match Expectations

1. Reject the fee plan
2. Explain your concerns
3. Discuss with arranger
4. Accept revised terms

### Documentation

Keep records of:
- Original terms discussed
- Fee plan received
- Any modifications
