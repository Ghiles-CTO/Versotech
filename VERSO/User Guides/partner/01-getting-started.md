# Partner Guide: Getting Started

Welcome to the Partner persona on Versotech. This guide introduces your role and capabilities.

![Partner Dashboard](./screenshots/partner-commissions-actual.png)
*The Partner dashboard with commission tracking and deal overview.*

---

## Overview

As a **Partner**, you have a strategic relationship with arrangers, typically involving:
- Co-arrangement of deals
- Broader investment partnerships
- Higher-tier commission structures
- Strategic collaboration

---

## Partner vs Introducer

| Aspect | Partner | Introducer |
|--------|---------|------------|
| **Relationship** | Strategic, long-term | Transactional |
| **Involvement** | May co-arrange | Referral only |
| **Commission** | Often higher/structured | Standard rates |
| **Agreement** | Partnership agreement | Introducer agreement |

---

## Accessing the Partner Dashboard

### Switching to Partner Persona

1. Click the **persona switcher** in top navigation
2. Select **"Partner"** from dropdown
3. Dashboard refreshes with partner view

## Your Capabilities

As a Partner, you can:
- Access deals you're assigned to
- Track partnership commissions
- Submit invoices for payment
- View deal progress and status

---

## Getting Started Steps

1. **Sign Partnership Agreement** - [See next guide](./02-partnership-agreement.md)
2. **Accept Fee Plans** - For specific deals
3. **Track Commissions** - Monitor your earnings
4. **Submit Invoices** - Get paid

---

## Key Concepts

### Fee Plans are Deal-Specific

Like introducers, your fee plans are per deal:
- Each deal has specific terms
- Accept before involvement
- Commission tracks automatically

### Commission Basis

Commission is always on **funded amount**:
- Actual investor capital
- Not management fees
- Clear calculation basis
