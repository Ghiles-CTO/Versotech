# CEO & Staff Guide: Reconciliation & Reporting

Access reports, track commissions, and maintain oversight.

---

## Overview

Reporting tools help you monitor operations, track commissions, and maintain financial oversight across the platform.

---

## Accessing Reports

### Navigation

1. Go to **Reports** in left sidebar
2. See available report types
3. Generate and download

---

## Available Reports

### Subscription Reports

| Report | Contents |
|--------|----------|

| **By Investor** | Activity per investor |

## Generating Reports

### Step 1: Select Report Type

1. Choose from available reports
2. Report parameters shown

### Step 2: Set Parameters

| Parameter | Options |
|-----------|---------|
| **Date Range** | Custom, this month, quarter, etc. |
| **Filters** | By deal, status, etc. |
| **Grouping** | How to organize data |

### Step 3: Generate

1. Click **"Generate Report"**
2. Report processes
3. View on screen or download

### Step 4: Export

Export options:
- PDF (formatted)
- Excel (detailed)
- CSV (raw data)
