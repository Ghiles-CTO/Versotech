# CEO & Staff Guide: Delegation

Delegate approval authority to staff members.

---

## Overview

Delegation allows CEO to assign approval authority to staff members, enabling efficient operations while maintaining oversight.

---

## Why Delegate

### Benefits

| Benefit | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Efficiency** | Faster processing |
| **Availability** | Not dependent on one person |
| **Scalability** | Handle more volume |
| **Development** | Staff capability building |

### When to Delegate

- Routine approvals
- Specific approval types
- Limited scope decisions
- Defined circumstances

---

## Accessing Delegation Settings

### Navigation

1. Go to **Settings** → **Delegation**
2. See current delegations
3. Manage as needed

---

## Creating a Delegation

### Step 1: Start Delegation

1. Click **"+ New Delegation"**
2. Delegation form opens

### Step 2: Select Delegate

Choose staff member:
- Must be active user
- Must have staff persona
- Consider their role

### Step 3: Define Scope

Specify what's delegated:

| Approval Type | Can Delegate |
|---------------|--------------|
| **KYC Approval** | Yes |
| **Interest Approval** | Yes |
| **Subscription Approval** | Yes |
| **Deal Close Approval** | Limited |
| **Data Room Extensions** | Yes |

### Step 4: Set Limits

Define boundaries:

| Limit | Example |
|-------|---------|
| **Amount** | Up to $1M subscriptions |
| **Deal Type** | Specific deals only |
| **Time Period** | Until specific date |

### Step 5: Activate

1. Review settings
2. Click **"Activate Delegation"**
3. Delegate notified
4. Authority effective immediately

---

## Delegation Types

### Full Delegation

All approval authority for a type:
- Any amount
- Any deal
- No restrictions

### Limited Delegation

Restricted authority:
- Amount limits
- Specific deals
- Specific time period

### Temporary Delegation

Time-bound delegation:
- Start date
- End date
- Auto-expires

---

## Managing Delegations

### Viewing Active Delegations

1. Go to **Settings** → **Delegation**
2. See all active delegations
3. Filter by delegate, type

### Modifying Delegation

1. Click on delegation
2. Edit settings
3. Save changes

### Revoking Delegation

1. Click on delegation
2. Click **"Revoke"**
3. Confirm
4. Authority removed immediately

---

## Delegate Actions

### How Delegates Operate

Delegates:
- See items in their approval queue
- Can approve within scope
- Actions logged under their name
- CEO retains oversight

### Escalation

If beyond scope:
- Delegate cannot approve
- Escalates to CEO
- Clear handoff

---

## Audit Trail

### Delegation Actions

All delegation tracked:
- When created
- By whom
- What scope
- When revoked

### Delegated Approvals

Each approval shows:
- Who approved
- Under what delegation
- Within what limits

---

## Best Practices

### Clear Scope

Define precisely:
- What's included
- What's excluded
- When to escalate

### Appropriate Limits

Set reasonable limits:
- Match delegate's experience
- Start limited, expand over time
- Maintain risk management

### Regular Review

Periodically:
- Audit active delegations
- Adjust as needed
- Revoke when appropriate

### Communication

Ensure:
- Delegate understands scope
- Escalation path clear
- Support available

## Limits and Guardrails

### Amount Limits

Common approach:
- Staff: Up to $500K
- Senior Staff: Up to $1M
- CEO: Unlimited

### Deal Restrictions

May limit to:
- Specific vehicles
- Specific deal types
- Specific arrangers

### Time Restrictions

Useful for:
- Vacation coverage
- Temporary absence
- Trial periods
