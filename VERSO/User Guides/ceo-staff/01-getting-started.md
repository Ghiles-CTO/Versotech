# CEO & Staff Guide: Getting Started

Welcome to the CEO/Staff persona on Versotech. This guide covers the operational oversight and approval capabilities.

![CEO Executive Dashboard](./screenshots/ceo-dashboard-real.png)
*The Executive Dashboard with real operational data and metrics.*

---

## Overview

As **CEO** or **Staff**, you have elevated privileges for:
- Approving key workflow stages
- Managing users and entities
- Overseeing operations
- Countersigning documents

---

## Role Hierarchy

### Staff Levels

| Role | Capabilities |
|------|--------------|
| **CEO** | All approvals, final authority, countersignatures |
| **Admin** | Most approvals, user management |
| **Operations** | Daily operations, some approvals |
| **Relationship Manager** | Client interactions, limited approvals |

### Delegation

CEO can delegate approval authority:
- To specific staff members
- For specific approval types
- With or without limits

## Approval Types

### What You Approve

| Approval | When Triggered |
|----------|----------------|
| **KYC** | Investor submits documents |
| **Interest** | Investor expresses interest |
| **Subscription** | Investor subscribes |
| **Deal Close** | Arranger requests close |
| **Data Room Extension** | Investor needs more time |

### Approval Queue

Go to **Approvals** to see:
- All pending items
- Filter by type
- Priority indicators
- Actions available

## Key Responsibilities

### CEO

- Final approval authority
- Countersignature on key documents
- Strategic oversight
- User access decisions

### Staff

- Process day-to-day approvals
- Manage operations
- Support users
- Handle exceptions
