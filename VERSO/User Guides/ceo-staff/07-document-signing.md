# CEO & Staff Guide: Document Signing

Complete countersignatures and official document signing.

---

## Overview

As CEO or authorized staff, you countersign documents on behalf of VERSO, including subscription agreements, certificates, and other official documents.

---

## Countersignature Role

### What You Sign

| Document | When |
|----------|------|
| **Subscription Agreements** | After investor signs |
| **NDAs** | After investor signs |
| **Certificates** | At deal close |
| **Amendments** | As needed |

### Why Countersignature

- Completes bilateral agreements
- Officially binds the fund/vehicle
- Provides legal execution
- Triggers next steps

---

## Accessing Signing Queue

### Navigation

1. Go to **Signing** or **Documents** → **Pending Signatures**
2. See documents awaiting your signature
3. Priority indicators shown

### Queue Information

For each document:
- Document type
- Related deal/investor
- Submission date
- Urgency

---

## The Signing Process

### Step 1: Access Document

1. Click on pending document
2. Document opens in viewer

### Step 2: Review Document

Before signing, verify:
- Document is complete
- Investor signatures present
- Terms are correct
- All fields filled

### Step 3: Apply Signature

1. Navigate to signature field
2. Click to sign
3. Choose method:
   - Draw
   - Type
   - Upload
4. Apply signature

### Step 4: Complete

1. Review signed document
2. Click "Complete Signing"
3. Document finalized
4. Parties notified

---

## After Signing

### What Happens

1. **Document completed**: Fully executed
2. **Copies distributed**: All parties receive
3. **Next steps triggered**: Based on document type
4. **System updated**: Status changes

### For Subscription Agreements

After countersigning:
- Document fully executed
- Wire instructions can be sent
- Investor proceeds to funding

### For Certificates

After signing:
- Certificates finalized
- Distributed to investors
- Stored in system

---

## Delegation

### Delegating Signature Authority

CEO can delegate signing to:
- Specific staff members
- For specific document types
- With defined limits

### Setting Up Delegation

1. Go to **Settings** → **Delegation**
2. Select staff member
3. Define scope
4. Activate delegation

### Delegated Signing

Delegates sign with:
- Clear authority
- Audit trail
- Same process

---

## Bulk Signing

### When Applicable

For multiple similar documents:
- Deal close with many investors
- Standard agreements
- Certificate batches

### Bulk Process

1. Select multiple documents
2. Review summary
3. Sign all at once
4. Each individually tracked

## Common Document Types

### NDA Countersignature

After investor signs NDA:
1. Review completeness
2. Countersign
3. Triggers data room access

### Subscription Agreement

After investor completes signing:
1. Verify all investor signatures
2. Countersign
3. Document fully executed

### Share/Unit Certificates

At deal close:
1. Certificates generated
2. Sign each certificate
3. Distributed to investors

## Best Practices

### Timely Signing

Process promptly:
- Investors waiting
- Deal timelines
- Service expectations

### Verification

Before signing:
- Document complete
- Required signatures present
- Terms accurate

### Secure Access

Protect your signing authority:
- Secure credentials
- Don't share access
- Review delegation regularly
