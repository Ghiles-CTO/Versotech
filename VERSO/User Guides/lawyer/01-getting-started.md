# Lawyer Guide: Getting Started

Welcome to the Lawyer persona on Versotech.

---

## Overview

As a **Lawyer** on Versotech, you provide legal services to vehicles and deals. Your role primarily involves:
- Being assigned to vehicles as legal counsel
- Signing certificates and legal documents
- Reviewing and certifying deal documentation

---

## Lawyer Role on the Platform

### What You Do

1. Receive vehicle assignments
2. Review legal documents
3. Sign certificates
4. Provide legal certifications

### Typical Tasks

| Task | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Certificate Signing** | Sign share/unit certificates |
| **Document Review** | Review subscription documents |
| **Legal Opinions** | Provide certifications |
| **Closing** | Participate in deal closings |

---

## Accessing the Lawyer Dashboard

### Switching to Lawyer Persona

1. Click persona switcher
2. Select "Lawyer"
3. Dashboard refreshes

### Dashboard Overview

| Section | Shows |
|---------|-------|
| **Pending Actions** | Documents awaiting signature |
| **Vehicles** | Vehicles you're assigned to |
| **Recent Activity** | Latest actions |

---

## Main Navigation

| Section | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| **Dashboard** | Overview and pending items |
| **Vehicles** | Vehicles you're legal counsel for |
| **Signing** | Documents awaiting your signature |
| **Documents** | Access to relevant documents |

---

## Your Capabilities

As a Lawyer:
- View vehicles you're assigned to
- Access relevant deal documents
- Sign certificates electronically
- Maintain signing history

---

## How You're Added

### Assignment Process

1. Arranger creates/configures vehicle
2. Assigns your firm as legal counsel
3. You receive notification
4. Vehicle appears in your dashboard

---

## Notifications

### What You're Notified About

| Event | Notification |
|-------|--------------|
| **Vehicle Assignment** | New vehicle added |
| **Signature Request** | Document needs signing |
| **Close Approaching** | Deal nearing close |
