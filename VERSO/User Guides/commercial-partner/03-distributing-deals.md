# Commercial Partner Guide: Distributing Deals

Access and share investment opportunities with your client network.

---

## Overview

As a Commercial Partner, you receive deals from arrangers and distribute them to your investor network. This guide covers how to access deals and connect your clients.

---

## Receiving Deals

### How Deals Come to You

1. Arranger dispatches deal to you
2. You receive notification
3. Deal appears in your Deals section
4. Fee plan sent for acceptance

### Before Distributing

Ensure:
- ✅ Placement agreement active
- ✅ Fee plan accepted for this deal
- ✅ Deal suitable for your clients

---

## Viewing Available Deals

### Deals Section

1. Go to **Deals**
2. See deals dispatched to you
3. Filter by status, sector

### Deal Information

For each deal:
- Summary and highlights
- Key terms
- Materials for sharing
- Fee plan status

---

## Accepting Fee Plans

### Required Before Distribution

Each deal has a fee plan:
1. Review terms
2. Accept to enable commission tracking
3. Then start distributing

### Fee Plan Process

1. Notification received
2. Review rate structure
3. Accept or negotiate
4. Proceed with distribution

---

## Connecting Clients

### Adding Client Investors

1. From deal, click "Add Investor"
2. Enter client details
3. Link to your distribution

### Client Journey

When your client expresses interest:
```
Your Client → Expresses Interest → Approved → NDA → Data Room → Subscribe → Fund
```

### Your Attribution

Clients you connect are attributed to you:
- Automatic tracking
- Commission on their funded amount

---

## Tracking Client Progress

### Transactions View

Go to **Transactions**:
- See all client subscriptions
- Status of each
- Amounts and progress

### Transaction Statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| `interested` | Client expressed interest |
| `pending` | Subscription submitted |
| `committed` | Approved, signing |
| `funded` | Money received |
| `active` | Deal closed |
