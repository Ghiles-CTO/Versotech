# Commercial Partner Guide: Placement Agreement

Sign your placement agreement to formalize the distribution relationship.

---

## Overview

The **Placement Agreement** establishes your formal relationship as a distribution partner. It defines your rights and obligations when distributing deals.

---

## What the Agreement Covers

### Key Sections

| Section | Content |
|---------|---------|
| **Scope** | Distribution rights and territories |
| **Obligations** | Your responsibilities |
| **Commission** | Framework for compensation |
| **Compliance** | Regulatory requirements |
| **Term** | Duration and renewal |
| **Termination** | Exit provisions |

---

## Receiving the Agreement

### Notification

When an arranger adds you:
1. Email with agreement link
2. Platform notification
3. Dashboard shows pending

---

## Reviewing Before Signing

### Important Considerations

| Area | What to Check |
|------|---------------|
| **Territory** | Where you can distribute |
| **Exclusivity** | Any exclusive rights/restrictions |
| **Commission** | Framework (details in fee plans) |
| **Compliance** | Your regulatory obligations |
| **Duration** | Agreement term |

### Legal Review

Consider having your legal team review:
- Distribution rights
- Liability provisions
- Termination terms

---

## Signing Process

### Step 1: Access Agreement

1. Click signing link
2. Review complete document

### Step 2: Sign

1. Navigate to signature fields
2. Apply signature
3. Complete additional fields

### Step 3: Submit

1. Review all entries
2. Click "Complete Signing"
3. Receive confirmation

---

## After Signing

### Immediate Effects

- Agreement active
- Can receive deal dispatches
- Can accept deal-specific fee plans
- Distribution activities enabled

### Document Access

Stored in **Documents**:
- Always accessible
- Reference for terms

---

## Agreement Status

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| `pending` | Awaiting signature |
| `active` | In effect |
| `expired` | Term ended |
| `terminated` | Relationship ended |
