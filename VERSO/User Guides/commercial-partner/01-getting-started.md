# Commercial Partner Guide: Getting Started

Welcome to the Commercial Partner persona on Versotech.

![Commercial Partner Dashboard](./screenshots/cp-dashboard-real.png)
*The Commercial Partner Dashboard with real client metrics, active opportunities, and commission earnings data.*

---

## Overview

As a **Commercial Partner**, you distribute investment opportunities to your investor network. This role is designed for:
- Wealth managers
- Family offices distributing to clients
- Distribution platforms
- Placement agents

---

## Commercial Partner Role

### What You Do

1. Receive deals from arrangers
2. Distribute to your client network
3. Facilitate investor onboarding
4. Track commissions on successful subscriptions

### Key Difference from Introducer

| Aspect | Commercial Partner | Introducer |
|--------|-------------------|------------|
| **Relationship** | Formal distribution agreement | Referral arrangement |
| **Network** | Established client base | Individual connections |
| **Process** | May handle more of investor journey | Typically referral only |
| **Structure** | Placement agreement | Introducer agreement |

---

## Accessing Your Dashboard

### Switching Persona

1. Click persona switcher
2. Select "Commercial Partner"
3. Dashboard refreshes

### Dashboard Overview

The Commercial Partner Dashboard shows your key business metrics:

| Card | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Clients Managed** | Number of active clients in your network |
| **Active Opportunities** | Deals currently being distributed |
| **Client Investments** | Total invested via proxy subscriptions |
| **Own Investments** | Your direct investments (if applicable) |

### Commission Earnings Section

Your earnings are broken down into:

| Status | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Paid** | Commissions already paid out |
| **Accrued** | Commissions earned, awaiting invoice |
| **Pending** | Commissions in process |

Additional metrics:
- **Total Subscriptions** (with pending count)
- **Active Placement Agreements** count

## Key Concepts

### Placement Agreement

Your formal distribution agreement covers:
- Scope of relationship
- Distribution rights
- Commission framework
- Compliance requirements

### Fee Plans

Like other partners, fee plans are deal-specific:
- Each deal has its terms
- Accept before distributing
- Commissions track automatically

### Commission Basis

Always on **funded amount**:
- What your clients actually invest
- Not management fees
- Clear calculation
