# Investor Guide: Portfolio Management

Monitor and manage your investment holdings on Versotech.

![Investor Portfolio](./screenshots/investor-portfolio.png)
*The Portfolio page shows all your investment positions across vehicles.*

---

## Overview

Once you have active positions, the Portfolio section becomes your central hub for:
- Viewing all holdings across vehicles
- Tracking performance and valuations
- Accessing documents and statements
- Managing distributions

---

## Accessing Your Portfolio

### Navigation

1. Ensure you're in **Investor** persona
2. Click **"Portfolio"** in the left sidebar
3. You'll see an overview of all positions

> **Investor Sidebar**: Dashboard, Investment Opportunities, Portfolio, Documents, Inbox, Profile

## Understanding Valuations

### NAV (Net Asset Value)

NAV represents the current fair value of your position:
- Updated according to fund schedule
- Reflects underlying asset valuations
- Basis for performance calculations

### Valuation Frequency

| Fund Type | Typical Frequency |
|-----------|-------------------|
| Real Estate | Quarterly |
| Private Equity | Quarterly/Semi-annually |
| Venture Capital | Semi-annually/Annually |
| Liquid Strategies | Monthly |

### Reading the Performance Chart

The performance chart shows:
- **Blue line**: Your position value over time
- **Dotted line**: Cost basis (what you paid)
- **Points**: Valuation dates
- Hover for specific values

## Distributions

### What Are Distributions?

Returns of capital or profits from your investment:
- Dividend payments
- Interest distributions
- Capital gains distributions
- Return of capital

### Tracking Distributions

In your position details:
1. Go to **Activity** tab
2. Filter by **"Distributions"**
3. See each distribution:
   - Date
   - Amount
   - Type
   - Payment status

### Receiving Distributions

1. **Notification** when distribution announced
2. **Bank details** confirmed (or requested)
3. **Payment** sent to your account
4. **Confirmation** in your activity

## Cash Flow Tracking

### Capital In

Track what you've invested:
- Original subscriptions
- Additional capital calls
- Reinvested distributions

### Capital Out

Track returns:
- Distributions received
- Return of capital
- Redemptions (if applicable)

### Net Cash Flow

The platform shows:
```
Net Cash Flow = Distributions Received - Capital Invested
```

## Performance Metrics

### IRR (Internal Rate of Return)

Time-weighted return on your investment, accounting for:
- When capital was invested
- When distributions received
- Current valuation

### Multiple on Invested Capital (MOIC)

```
MOIC = (Current Value + Distributions) / Capital Invested
```

Example:
- Invested: $100,000
- Current Value: $80,000
- Distributions: $40,000
- MOIC = 1.2x

## Common Questions

### "My NAV hasn't updated in a while"

NAV updates follow fund schedule:
- Check the "Last Updated" date
- Quarterly funds update 4x per year
- Contact investor relations if concerned

### "I can't find a document"

1. Check all tabs in the position
2. Use the search function
3. Check the entity-level documents
4. Contact investor relations

### "My return looks wrong"

Check that you're viewing:
- Correct time period
- Including/excluding distributions
- Right entity
- Performance is NAV-based (not realized)

### "How do I update my bank details?"

1. Go to **Profile** → **Banking Information**
2. Update your details
3. Verification may be required
4. New distributions use updated info
