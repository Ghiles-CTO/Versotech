# Investor Guide: Profile Setup

Complete your investor profile to unlock access to investment opportunities on Versotech.

---

## Overview

Before you can express interest in deals or subscribe to investments, you need to:
1. Complete your personal profile
2. Add at least one investment entity (if investing through a company/trust)
3. Verify your contact information

![Investor Dashboard](./screenshots/investor-dashboard.png)
*Your investor dashboard shows open opportunities, outstanding tasks, and active holdings.*

---

## Completing Your Personal Profile

### Step 1: Access Profile Settings

1. Click **"Profile"** in the left sidebar
2. You'll land on your profile page with personal information

![Investor Profile](./screenshots/investor-profile.png)
*The investor profile page where you manage your personal and entity information.*

### Step 2: Required Information

Fill in the following fields:

| Field | Description | Required |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| Full Name | Your legal name as it appears on documents | Yes |
| Email | Primary contact email | Yes |
| Phone | Mobile number with country code | Yes |
| Country of Residence | Where you're tax resident | Yes |
| Nationality | Your citizenship | Yes |
| Date of Birth | For identity verification | Yes |

### Step 3: Additional Details

Some optional but recommended fields:
- **LinkedIn Profile** - Helps arrangers verify your background
- **Professional Bio** - Brief description of your investment experience
- **Investment Focus** - Sectors or asset classes you prefer

### Step 4: Save Your Profile

Click **"Save Changes"** at the bottom of the form.

## Adding Investment Entities

Most sophisticated investors invest through entities (companies, trusts, family offices) rather than personally. Adding entities allows you to:
- Choose which entity subscribes to each deal
- Manage different investment strategies per entity
- Maintain proper legal separation

### Creating a New Entity

1. Navigate to **Profile** â†’ **My Entities** tab
2. Click **"+ Add Entity"**
3. Complete the entity form:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Entity Name | Legal registered name |
| Entity Type | Company, Trust, Family Office, LP, etc. |
| Jurisdiction | Country/state of registration |
| Registration Number | Company/Trust number |
| Registered Address | Official address on file |
| Tax ID | VAT/EIN/equivalent |

### Adding Entity Members

For entity investments, you'll need to specify:
- **Authorized Signatories** - Who can sign on behalf of the entity
- **Beneficial Owners** - Ultimate ownership structure (for KYC)
- **Directors/Trustees** - Governing body members

#### To Add Members:
1. After creating the entity, click **"Manage Members"**
2. Click **"+ Add Member"**
3. Enter member details:
   - Full name
   - Role (Director, Signatory, UBO, etc.)
   - Email (for signature requests)
   - Ownership percentage (if applicable)

> **Important**: Entity investments require signatures from ALL authorized signatories. Make sure you add everyone who needs to sign.

---

## Multi-Signatory Considerations

If your entity has multiple signatories:
- All signatories will receive signature requests
- Documents won't progress until **every** signatory has signed
- Each signatory needs their own Versotech account

### Example: Company with 2 Directors

Both directors must:
1. Have accounts on Versotech
2. Be listed as signatories on the entity
3. Sign every subscription document

## Common Issues

### "I can't see any investment opportunities"

1. Make sure your profile is complete (100% progress)
2. Check if you need to complete KYC first
3. Verify you're logged in with the **Investor** persona

### "My entity isn't showing when I try to subscribe"

1. Ensure the entity has at least one signatory
2. Check that the entity has completed KYC
3. Verify the entity is marked as "Active"
