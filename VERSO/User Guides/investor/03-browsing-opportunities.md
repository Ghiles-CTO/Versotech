# Investor Guide: Browsing Investment Opportunities

Discover and explore available investment opportunities on Versotech.

---

## Overview

As an approved investor, you can browse deals that are:
- **Open** for new interest
- Matched to your investor profile (if applicable)
- Shared with you by arrangers or introducers

![Investment Opportunities Page](./screenshots/investor-opportunities.png)
*The Investment Opportunities page shows all available deals with filtering options.*

---

## Accessing Opportunities

### Step 1: Navigate to Investment Opportunities

1. Ensure you're logged in with the **Investor** persona
2. Click **"Investment Opportunities"** in the left sidebar
3. You'll see a page with summary cards and a filterable list of deals

### Summary Cards

At the top of the page, you'll see status cards:

| Card | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Open deals** | Deals ready for exploration |
| **Pending interests** | Your interests awaiting team review |
| **Active NDAs** | Deals where your data room is unlocked |
| **Subscriptions in review** | Your subscriptions awaiting confirmation |

### Step 2: Search and Filter

Use the search bar and filters to find relevant deals:

| Filter | Options |
|--------|---------|
| **Search** | Search deals by name, company, sector, location |
| **All Status** | Filter by deal status (Open, Closed) |
| **All Types** | Filter by investment type |
| **All Stages** | Filter by deal stage (Series A, Series B, etc.) |
| **Closing Date** | Sort by closing date |

Click **"Filters"** for advanced filtering options.

---

## Understanding Deal Cards

Each deal card shows key information at a glance:

### Header Section
- **Company Logo** - Visual identifier for the company
- **Deal Name** - The company/investment name (e.g., "Perplexity", "Anthropic")
- **Status Badge** - Current deal status (OPEN, CLOSED)
- **Category Badge** - Investment sector (e.g., AI, Primary Equity)

### Key Metrics
| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Stage** | Investment stage (Series A, Series B, etc.) |
| **Sector** | Industry sector (AI, Real Estate, etc.) |
| **Location** | Geographic location |
| **ALLOCATION** | Total raise amount |
| **MINIMUM TICKET** | Minimum investment amount |
| **UNIT PRICE** | Price per unit/share |
| **TIMELINE** | Closing date |

### Your Pipeline Status
Each deal shows where you are in the investment journey:
- **No signal yet** - Haven't expressed interest
- **Tracking only** - Watching but not committed
- Interest deadline displayed for open deals

### Quick Actions
- **View details** - Opens full deal detail page
- **Visit company site** - External link to company website (when available)

---

## Filtering Opportunities

Use filters to find relevant deals:

### Available Filters

| Filter | Options |
|--------|---------|
| Status | Open, Closing Soon, Fully Subscribed |
| Sector | Real Estate, Private Equity, Venture, etc. |
| Geography | By investment location |
| Investment Range | Filter by minimum amount |
| Vehicle | Filter by specific fund |

### Applying Filters

1. Click the **"Filter"** button above the deal list
2. Select your criteria
3. Click **"Apply Filters"**
4. Clear filters with **"Reset"**

---

## Deal Detail Page

Click on any deal to see complete information:

### Overview Tab
- Executive summary
- Investment thesis
- Key highlights
- Fundraising progress bar

### Term Sheet Tab
- Investment terms
- Fee structure (management fees, carried interest)
- Distribution waterfall
- Key dates

### Documents Tab
- Teaser/pitch deck (if publicly available)
- Note: Full documents require data room access

### Key Details Card
Shows structured information:
- Sector and geography
- Target returns
- Investment period
- Currency

## Deal Statuses Explained

| Status | Meaning | Can You Invest? |
|--------|---------|-----------------|
| `draft` | Not yet published | No |
| `open` | Accepting interest | Yes |
| `allocation_pending` | Interest closed, allocating | Maybe (waitlist) |
| `fully_subscribed` | Fully committed | No |
| `closed` | Deal complete | No |

## Watchlist Feature

Save deals you're interested in:

1. Click the **bookmark icon** on any deal card
2. Access your watchlist from **Opportunities** → **Watchlist** tab
3. Get notified when watchlisted deals have updates

## Deal Matching

Some deals may be specifically matched to you based on:
- Your investment preferences
- Your introducer relationships
- Direct sharing by arrangers

Look for the **"Matched for You"** badge on relevant deals.

## Understanding What You See vs. What's Available

### Why Some Deals Don't Appear

Not all deals are visible to all investors:
- Some are **private** (by invitation only)
- Some require **specific KYC status**
- Some are **geography-restricted**
- Some are only for **qualified/accredited investors**

### Getting Access to More Deals

1. Complete your KYC fully
2. Update your investment preferences
3. Connect with introducers who can share opportunities
4. Contact VERSO to discuss access

## Common Questions

### "I was told about a deal but can't see it"

The deal may be:
1. Not yet published (ask the arranger)
2. Private access only (request access)
3. Closed to new investors

### "The fundraising numbers don't match what I heard"

Progress updates in real-time as subscriptions are submitted and funded. Numbers reflect current state.

### "Can I see deals from all arrangers?"

You see deals where:
- You have explicit access
- The deal is publicly listed
- Your introducer has connected you
