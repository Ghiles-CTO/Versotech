## üîÑ Business Logic Integration

### No-Oversell Protection
- ‚úÖ `fn_reserve_inventory` uses `FOR UPDATE SKIP LOCKED`
- ‚úÖ FIFO allocation across share lots (oldest first)
- ‚úÖ Atomic operations prevent race conditions
- ‚úÖ Reservation expiry restores units via `fn_expire_reservations`

### Commitment ‚Üí Reservation ‚Üí Allocation Flow
1. Investor submits commitment (investor portal)
2. Staff reviews in Commitments tab
3. Staff approves ‚Üí **calls `fn_reserve_inventory`**
   - Locks inventory units across lots
   - Creates reservation with 48h expiry
   - Decrements `share_lots.units_remaining`
4. Staff finalizes ‚Üí **calls `fn_finalize_allocation`**
   - Creates allocation record
   - Updates `positions` table
   - Generates invoices for fees and spread
   - Creates approval records

### Deal-Scoped Access
- ‚úÖ Documents filtered by `deal_id`
- ‚úÖ Conversations filtered by `deal_id`
- ‚úÖ Members see only their deals via `deal_memberships`
- ‚úÖ RLS policies enforce isolation
